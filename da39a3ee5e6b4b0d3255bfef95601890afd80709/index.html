Context

Why you're coming back

This isn't a standard onboarding. You know the team, the stack, and what Displate is trying to become. What's changed is the mandate: Data now sits inside the AI function, and that function is company-wide — not a service layer for one department. You'll have a home in Growth, but the remit spans every team that has something to gain from moving faster with AI. This document makes the implicit explicit: what you own, what the 90 days look like, and how we'll know this is working.

The role

The opportunity

Displate has the data infrastructure, a growing influencer and performance marketing operation, and a team that's ready to move. What's been missing is someone who can connect the dots between raw capability and AI-driven execution — and do it hands-on, at pace, across the whole company. This role is that connective tissue.

Cross-company mandate
Reporting to the CGO, this role has license to work across Growth, Product, Marketing, and Operations. The goal is not to build a department — it's to embed AI capability wherever it creates the most leverage, fast.
Responsibilities

What you own

Data infrastructure & analytics

The foundation

  • Own the data team's delivery cadence and technical direction
  • Stay hands-on where speed matters — no over-delegation
  • Maintain the data model underpinning all company reporting
  • Drive pipeline reliability and documentation across BigQuery
Marketing attribution & performance

The signal layer

  • Own attribution strategy across paid, influencer, and organic
  • Make incrementality testing a repeatable, trusted practice
  • Deliver weekly actionable insight to CGO and performance teams
  • Turn measurement into decision-making, not just reporting
AI tooling & automation

The multiplier

  • Build and ship AI workflows that reduce manual work company-wide
  • Identify highest-ROI automation targets across every function
  • Grow company-wide AI proficiency through tools, not just training
  • Evaluate and integrate LLM tooling into core workflows
Leadership & culture

The team

  • Directly lead and stabilise the data team from day one
  • Be the person the team builds toward, not around
  • Represent AI capability at leadership level with clarity and confidence
  • Create conditions for the team to do their best work
This engagement will not work if…
  • Delivery is delegated rather than owned
  • Strategy outpaces execution in the first 60 days
  • The team doesn't feel the difference within a month
  • AI work stays inside the data team instead of spreading out
